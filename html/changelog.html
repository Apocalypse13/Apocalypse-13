07 November 2020

ATH1909 updated:

  • An issue with the UNREAL SORD's suicide text and effect has been fixed.

ArcaneMusic updated:

  • Newscasters internal scanners have been updated, and now correctly obtain crewmembers ID cards from their wallets/ID holding devices.
  • Floorbots had had a software update, preventing them from dogpiling on their target as easily as they did before.
  • Floorbots will now play a small chime when stacked on top of each other to indicate that they're moving apart.

Fikou updated:

  • You will no longer lose the mission if you go to water plants on Ice Box, comrade.

Ghommie updated:

  • You can now unbuckle others from the toolbox training machine with altclick.
  • Fixing GBJ cases where the disposal holder (the sctructure holding and moving all that was flushed around the pipenet) is forcemoved outside a disposal pipe.
  • Non-human mobs now also benefit from held id cards and some economy features (dexterity checks still apply).
  • Mechas will once again fall/walk downstair.
  • No more clickdragging one person to another that isn't you doesn't opening inventory windows.

IndieanaJones updated:

  • The Spider Clan has recently taken up the Space Ninja project again along with the Syndicate. Space Ninjas have been drastically changed as a result, becoming much weaker and more stealth oriented. As a result of cutting costs per ninja, more ninjas were able to be hired. Expect to see them around more often.

LemonInTheDark updated:

  • Catwalks will now properly only smooth with other catwalks

Ryll/Shaps updated:

  • Improvised bone wound treatments with bone gel and surgical tape applied outside of surgery no longer take ridiculous amounts of time, down from 10/20 minutes for severe/critical bone wounds to 4/8 minutes.
  • Sleeping while an improvised bone wound treatment is processing will cause it to heal about 50% faster, while laying down while awake will heal it about 25% faster. Your bones will spike with pain a bit more frequently during the healing though.

Skoglol updated:

  • Proper capitalization of RCD/RPD techs

Timberpoes updated:

  • Gondolas no longer turn invisible when given a pet collar.
  • Fixes weird lighting issues when crafting items with component parts that have lighting components.
  • Personal AI emitters now regenerate at their properly intended speed instead of 1/10th of that speed.

alphanerdd updated:

  • Made Nothing the cure for Gluttony's Blessing instead of Adminordazine.
  • Fixed the cure text so it both no longer displays a DM path, and also displays the correct cure.

cacogen updated:

  • Anyone can now download BotKeeper (tablet app). Only bots your current ID can unlock will be listed to control.

dragomagol updated:

  • Added lights to Metastation telecomms storage

necromanceranne updated:

  • Reworks the speargun BACK to being a gun! Complete with embedding harpoons! How exciting.
  • Adds new mob sprites for the harpoon gun and a suit storage sprite for the mosin nagant. Also adds a new quiver sprite.

prodirus updated:

  • Roundstart Centcomm reports on communication consoles should no longer be word soup!

05 November 2020

Ghilker updated:

  • Hypernoblium stops reaction only from 20 K and up
  • Hypernoblium formation has been changed to be under 15 K and it requires a lot of BZ as catalyst to lower the energy released by the reaction.
  • Lowered research points amount per moles from 1000 to 30
  • Hypernobilium export price lowered to 5

Ghommie updated:

  • Ripley mechas will now display the right overlays for their armor plates.

IndieanaJones updated:

  • Networked Fiber blobs can now place down tiles with their nodes along with their cores and have the same movement effect as well.

Krysonism updated:

  • fixed a gulag stacking machine exploit that would let you farm points with minimal or no ores mined.

Skoglol updated:

  • Immovable rods should more consistently hit the station, will reset target if adminbused, and added more logs.
  • Stuffing people into crates, closets and pods works again.

XDTM updated:

  • Spreading nanite programs (Exo-locomotion and Nanite Sting) now properly transfer the infector's cloud ID to the infectee.

04 November 2020

Qustinnus updated:

  • All turfs now support transparency
  • Datum material floors and walls now support transparency
  • Glass floors are now destructible

necromanceranne updated:

  • Removes the now depreciated high-priority cargo bounty event, that does nothing.
